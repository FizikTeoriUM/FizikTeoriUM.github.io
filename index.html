Welcome to
Theoretical Physics Group

A curious mind knows no limit

Established in 1999 by Professor Hasan Abu Kassim focusing on the theoretical low energy nuclear physics and now superseded by Dr Norhasliza Yusof starting from November 2021. Our current research focus are on theoretical astrophysics and neutrino astrophysics particularly in evolution of massive stars.

Our research group located at Theoretical Physics Lab, Block C at Department of Physics, Universiti Malaya
